Interior Design Plans by Alex

Below are four ready-to-adapt design plans. Each plan includes a clear concept, a layout direction, and a palette/material strategy you can use as-is or customize for your space.

Featured plans

Plan 01 • Living Room

Sunlit Scandinavian Living Room

Create an airy, family-friendly space with warm woods, soft neutrals, and layered lighting.

Deliverables: layout direction, palette, lighting plan, and a starter shopping list.

Plan 02 • Bedroom

Calm Japandi Bedroom Retreat

Balance minimal lines and cozy texture using warm beige, oak, linen, and gentle contrast.

Plan 03 • Kitchen

Modern Warm Kitchen Refresh

Upgrade a practical kitchen with matte finishes, warm metals, and easy-to-clean surfaces.

Compact Home Office Nook

Make a small corner work hard: focused task lighting, acoustics, and tidy cable strategy.

Deliverables: layout, storage plan, desk/shelving sizing tips, and a focused palette.
